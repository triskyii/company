WHAT MAKES OUR COFFEE DIFFERENT

A cup of coffee contains about 7 mg, which is a drop in the daily-requirement bucket (420 mg for men, 320 mg for women). But because we don't eat enough fruit, vegetables, and whole grains, the average American's intake falls about 100 mg short of the daily goal.
